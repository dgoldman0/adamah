# **SECTION A — Multiple Choice (20 questions)**

Pick one answer for each.

### **Tools & Handling**

1. Which tool helps prevent a workpiece from shifting while cutting or drilling?
   a) Utility knife b) Clamp c) File d) Tape measure

2. A pilot hole is used to:
   a) Hide a screw b) Guide a screw and reduce splitting c) Store spare screws d) Measure depth

3. A bit with a sharp brad point is designed for:
   a) Metal b) Wood c) Plastic d) Concrete

4. A Torx driver is shaped to reduce:
   a) Paint chipping b) Cam-out under torque c) Wire stripping d) Bearing friction

5. A rotary tool excels at:
   a) Heavy lumber cuts b) Fine shaping, sanding, grinding c) Concrete drilling d) Bending sheet metal

### **Electrical & Electronic Basics**

6. In a simple circuit with a battery, switch, and LED, the LED won’t light. First check:
   a) LED orientation
   b) Color of wire insulation
   c) Label on the switch
   d) Screw length in the enclosure

7. A multimeter set to “continuity” determines:
   a) LED brightness
   b) Whether two points are connected
   c) Battery lifespan
   d) Resistor color code

8. A resistor in series with an LED serves to:
   a) Change LED polarity
   b) Limit current to safe levels
   c) Connect ground
   d) Amplify voltage

9. A fuse protects a circuit by:
   a) Increasing voltage
   b) Opening the circuit during overcurrent
   c) Providing a switch
   d) Regulating temperature

10. Heat-shrink tubing is used for:
    a) Cooling wires
    b) Encapsulating LEDs
    c) Insulating and reinforcing wire joints
    d) Adjusting voltage output

### **Mechanical & Materials**

11. A loose gear in a small mechanism usually points to:
    a) Lack of lubrication
    b) Incorrect alignment or missing fastener
    c) Gear color change
    d) Oversized motor

12. A lever increases force by adjusting:
    a) Gear ratio
    b) Fulcrum position
    c) Wire gauge
    d) Voltage rating

13. Wood typically drills cleaner when:
    a) Using high feed pressure
    b) Using a sharp bit and backing board
    c) Using a metal bit at low speed
    d) Using no pilot hole

14. Aluminum is favored because it:
    a) Resists all corrosion
    b) Is lightweight and machines smoothly
    c) Is magnetic
    d) Requires no cutting tools

15. A bearing reduces:
    a) Load capacity
    b) Friction between rotating parts
    c) Wire strain
    d) Screw thread wear

### **Safety & Good Practice**

16. Eye protection is especially important when:
    a) Measuring materials
    b) Cutting, grinding, or drilling
    c) Marking layouts
    d) Sorting hardware

17. Ventilation matters during:
    a) Filing metal
    b) Soldering or using strong adhesives
    c) Reading schematics
    d) Handling tape measures

18. When sanding wood, higher-grit sandpaper means:
    a) Rougher surface
    b) Faster material removal
    c) Finer finish
    d) No dust generated

19. A stripped screw head often results from:
    a) Using a well-matched bit
    b) Applying steady downward pressure
    c) Using the wrong driver size
    d) Using a pilot hole

20. Threadlocker (blue) is used to:
    a) Seal electrical joints
    b) Keep fasteners from loosening under vibration
    c) Increase torque on screws
    d) Lubricate bolts
